Swanston Street Detective

After 'the incident' people keep losing valuables in all that dang grass, can you crack these cases?

This game is meant to be played in person on the mean streets of Swanston st in Melbourne.

Follow the clues and once you're in the right area then it turns into a "where's wally/waldo" game of find the object in the grass.

This game requires access to your location but nothing leaves your device and there's no analytics or nasty privacy invading stuff, source code for this game can be found here

Good luck Detective!
